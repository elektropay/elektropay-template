Features / Deposits

Get paid

Your way

There are several ways to deposit money into your monese account. For customers in the UK Monese’s current accounts come with an account number and sort code so you can receive UK bank transfers from individuals or companies and even your regular salary / wages direct from an employer.

Bank transfers

From outside the UK

If you are outside of the UK you can still make payments into your UK Monese account via our European IBAN. Simply make a bank transfer to our European IBAN account and include your Monese ID as a payment reference, so we can do currency conversion at the real exchange rate (getting you the best possible exchange rate) and route your funds as GBP to your UK Monese account.

Deposit cash

Across the UK

You can deposit cash at over 40,000 locations across the UK and you don’t even need to enter a bank to do it. We’ve partnered with the Post Office and PayPoint to enable our customers to use any Post Office brand or high-street convenience store, corner-store or off-license displaying the PayPoint logo to deposit cash direct to their Monese account instantly.

Post Office

Using the Post Office to deposit

Just ask to top up your Monese Mastercard at the Post Office counter, hand the cash to the cashier and insert your Monese debit card into the chip and pin reader to have the money credited to your Monese account the next business day.

PayPoint

Using PayPoint to deposit

Just ask to use PayPoint to load your Monese debit card at the counter, then present your Monese card and the cash you wish to deposit.
