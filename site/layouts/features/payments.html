Features / Payments

Make payments

On your mobile

The Monese app makes it easy to pay for goods and services like rent or utility bills. With a few taps you can pay a company or individual recipient via bank transfer. Monese to Monese payments are free and happen in real time, with no limit on how many transfers you can make

Bank

Transfers

If you like staying in total control of your budget, bank transfers are a great way to pay. From essential services like your mobile phone and home broadband, to utilities like electricity, gas and water - paying via bank transfer allows you to review your bills and ensure they are accurate before making payment. For this reason, many people prefer bank transfers to Direct Debits, as signing up to Direct Debit enables the supplier to automatically charge you a variable amount.

Direct

Debits

You can set up a Direct Debit to automatically pay for the services and utilities you use regularly. Mobile phone contracts, rent, electricity bills and gym membership can all be paid for with Direct Debit.

Transfer

Money abroad

As a Monese account holder you can transfer money abroad into eight currencies - for up to 8x less than using a bank. We process millions of pounds worth of transactions every day and this huge volume means that we are able to buy currency at wholesale rates - and we pass these great rates on to our customers. There isn't a better way to send money abroad cheaply.

Pay by debit card

Or in cash

You can pay for a whole range of things with your Monese debit card. You can shop in store or online, make purchases in-app, or take cash out at ATMs globally.
